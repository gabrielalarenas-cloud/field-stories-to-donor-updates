# Demo Script: English
## From Field Update to Donor Story

## Suggested length

75 to 100 seconds.

## Script

Every week, nonprofit field teams collect the stories that help donors understand what their support makes possible.
But the people closest to the work are often the busiest people in the organization.
They are running programs, coordinating with local partners, supporting communities, solving problems, and adapting to changing conditions on the ground.
When headquarters needs photos, videos, testimonials, translations, and donor updates, the process can create delays, extra work, and tension between field teams and communications teams.
This proof of concept explores a different workflow.
A field coordinator records a short update in their own language using a phone.
HQ receives the update in a structured workflow. ElevenLabs supports transcription, translation, narration, and multilingual production, while the NGO keeps human review in place for consent, safety, and accuracy.
The result is not automated storytelling.
It is human-reviewed storytelling made faster, more accessible, and easier to scale.
For field teams, it reduces the burden of producing polished communications.
For headquarters, it creates a repeatable workflow.
For donors, it provides clearer and more timely updates.
And for the communities represented in the story, it keeps consent, dignity, and accuracy at the center.
For a global NGO, the value is not just one video. It is a repeatable workflow that can scale across programs, languages, and donor markets.
This is a small example of how ElevenLabs AI voice tools can help social impact organizations communicate across languages without losing the human context behind the work.
One field update. One responsible workflow. Multiple languages. Clearer donor trust.
