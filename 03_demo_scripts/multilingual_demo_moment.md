# Multilingual Demo Moment
## Preserving meaning and emotional consistency across languages

## Purpose

This short segment is designed for the middle or end of the demo video.

It shows how the same donor message could be delivered across languages while preserving tone, clarity, and emotional consistency.

## Important note

The Swahili line below is a draft sample for proof-of-concept purposes only. It should be reviewed by a native speaker before any real use.

## English

Every community voice deserves to be heard with dignity, accuracy, and care.

## Spanish

Cada voz de la comunidad merece ser escuchada con dignidad, precisión y cuidado.

## French

Chaque voix de la communauté mérite d'être entendue avec dignité, exactitude et attention.

## Swahili draft for demo review

Kila sauti ya jamii inapaswa kusikilizwa kwa heshima, usahihi na uangalifu.

## How to present this moment

When playing this section, call out that the value is not just translation.

The value is emotional consistency across contexts:

- The message remains respectful
- The tone remains calm and credible
- The donor receives the update in a language they understand
- The NGO keeps human review in place
- The local story is not flattened into generic campaign language

## Suggested voice direction

Use the same narrator persona across languages if the output remains natural.

If one voice performs better in one language than another, prioritize language quality and cultural fit over forcing one voice across every language.

## Review requirement

Any real partner deployment should include review by a fluent or native speaker before publication.
